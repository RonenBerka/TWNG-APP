## üî¢ Serial Number Dating

### Format (1993-present)

**YYMMDDXXX**
- YY = Year
- MM = Month
- DD = Day
- XXX = Production number

**Example:**
- 20071523 = 2020, July 15, #23 that day

### Older Formats

| Era | Format |
|-----|--------|
| 1974-1993 | Various, sequential |
| 1993+ | Date-based (above) |

### Serial Location

- Inside body, visible through soundhole
- On label inside guitar

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. USA vs Mexico

**Check:**
- Series number (100-400 mostly Mexico)
- Serial format (same)
- "Made in Mexico" vs "Made in USA" label

### 2. V-Class vs X-Bracing

**V-Class tells:**
- 2018 or later
- Usually noted on label
- Higher series models first

### 3. ES-1 vs ES2

**ES2 identifiers:**
- No soundhole controls
- Body-mounted preamp
- 2014 or later
