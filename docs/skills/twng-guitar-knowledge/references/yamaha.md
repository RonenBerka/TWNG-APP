## üî¢ Serial Number Decoding

### Modern Format (varies)

**Japan (L Series, Premium):**
- Often includes year indicators
- QJ = Japan quality

**Indonesia:**
- H = Indonesia (common)
- First digits often year

### Vintage Yamaha

| Era | Format |
|-----|--------|
| 1970s | Sequential, Japan |
| 1980s | Letter+numbers |
| 1990s+ | Factory+date codes |

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. Japan vs Indonesia

**Check:**
- Label inside soundhole
- Serial prefix
- Build quality details

### 2. Vintage FG Value

**Red Label FG tells:**
- 1966-1972
- "Nippon Gakki" label
- Highly sought after

### 3. Pacifica Tiers

**Quality order:**
- 612/611 (Japan) - Premium
- 311/312 (Indonesia) - Mid
- 112 (Indonesia) - Entry (excellent value)
- 012 (China) - Starter
