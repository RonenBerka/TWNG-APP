## âš ï¸ Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. Era Identification

**Westerly (RI) era most valued:**
- 1969-2001
- Look for Westerly serial ranges
- Premium quality

### 2. USA vs Import

**USA tells:**
- Made in USA label
- Higher price ($2,000+)
- Premium materials

**Import tells:**
- Westerly Collection = China
- Newark St. = Korea/China
- Good quality at price

### 3. Model Numbers

**Number meanings:**
- Higher = more appointments
- 55 > 50 > 47 > 40
- Letter = body shape (F, D, OM)
