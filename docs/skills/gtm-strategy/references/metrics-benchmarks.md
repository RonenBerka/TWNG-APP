# Metrics & Benchmarks

Industry benchmarks for common GTM metrics.

---

## Acquisition Metrics

### Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)

| Industry | B2C CPA | B2B CPA |
|----------|---------|---------|
| SaaS | $50-200 | $200-500 |
| E-commerce | $10-50 | N/A |
| Fintech | $50-150 | $300-800 |
| Healthcare | $30-100 | $200-600 |
| Education | $20-80 | $150-400 |

### Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) by Channel

| Channel | Avg CAC | Notes |
|---------|---------|-------|
| Organic Search | $50-150 | Long-term investment |
| Paid Search | $100-300 | High intent |
| Paid Social | $50-200 | Varies by targeting |
| Email | $10-50 | Existing list |
| Referral | $20-80 | Usually lowest |
| Content | $50-200 | Long payback |

### Conversion Rate Benchmarks

| Funnel Stage | B2C | B2B |
|--------------|-----|-----|
| Visit → Lead | 2-5% | 1-3% |
| Lead → MQL | N/A | 20-40% |
| MQL → SQL | N/A | 30-50% |
| SQL → Opportunity | N/A | 50-70% |
| Opportunity → Close | N/A | 20-30% |
| Visit → Purchase | 1-3% | 0.5-2% |

## Retention Metrics

### Churn Benchmarks

| Business Type | Monthly Churn | Annual Churn |
|---------------|---------------|--------------|
| B2B SaaS | 2-5% | 10-30% |
| B2C SaaS | 5-10% | 40-60% |
| E-commerce | 20-30%* | N/A |
| Subscription Box | 10-15% | 50-70% |

*E-commerce measures repeat purchase rate instead

### Net Promoter Score (NPS)

| Score Range | Rating |
|-------------|--------|
| -100 to 0 | Poor |
| 0 to 30 | Average |
| 30 to 50 | Good |
| 50 to 70 | Excellent |
| 70+ | World-class |

### Activation Benchmarks

| Product Type | Day 1 Retention | Day 7 | Day 30 |
|--------------|-----------------|-------|--------|
| Mobile App | 25-35% | 10-15% | 5-10% |
| SaaS | 40-60% | 30-40% | 20-30% |
| E-commerce | 20-30%* | 10-15%* | 5-10%* |

*Repeat visit/purchase rate

## Revenue Metrics

### LTV:CAC Ratio

| Ratio | Interpretation |
|-------|----------------|
| <1:1 | Losing money |
| 1:1 - 2:1 | Unsustainable |
| 3:1 | Healthy |
| 4:1 - 5:1 | Strong |
| >5:1 | Under-investing in growth |

### Payback Period

| Business Type | Target Payback |
|---------------|----------------|
| B2B SaaS | <12 months |
| B2C SaaS | <6 months |
| E-commerce | <3 months |
| Marketplace | <6 months |

### Revenue per Employee

| Stage | Target |
|-------|--------|
| Seed | $100-200K |
| Series A | $150-250K |
| Series B+ | $200-400K |
| Public SaaS | $300-500K |
