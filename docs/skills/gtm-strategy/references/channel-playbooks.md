# Channel Playbooks

Tactical guides for each marketing channel.

---

## Organic Social

### Instagram
- **Best for**: B2C, visual products, lifestyle brands
- **Content mix**: 40% value, 30% engagement, 20% product, 10% promo
- **Frequency**: 4-7 posts/week, daily stories
- **KPIs**: Engagement rate (3%+ good), reach, saves, shares
- **Tactics**: Reels for reach, carousels for saves, stories for engagement

### LinkedIn
- **Best for**: B2B, professional services, SaaS
- **Content mix**: 50% insights, 30% stories, 20% company updates
- **Frequency**: 3-5 posts/week
- **KPIs**: Impressions, engagement, profile visits, connection requests
- **Tactics**: Personal profiles outperform company pages 10x

### TikTok
- **Best for**: B2C, younger demographics, trend-driven products
- **Content mix**: 70% entertainment, 20% education, 10% product
- **Frequency**: 1-3 posts/day for growth
- **KPIs**: Views, completion rate, shares, follows
- **Tactics**: Hook in first 1 second, trends over polish

### Twitter/X
- **Best for**: Tech, news, thought leadership, community
- **Content mix**: 40% commentary, 30% threads, 20% engagement, 10% promo
- **Frequency**: 3-10 tweets/day
- **KPIs**: Impressions, engagement rate, follower growth
- **Tactics**: Threads for depth, quotes for reach

## Paid Acquisition

### Meta Ads (Facebook/Instagram)
- **Best for**: B2C, e-commerce, lead gen
- **Budget start**: $50-100/day minimum for learning
- **Structure**: CBO campaigns, 3-5 ad sets, 3-6 ads per set
- **Targeting**: Start broad, let algorithm optimize
- **KPIs**: CPM, CTR, CPA, ROAS
- **Benchmarks**: CPM $5-15, CTR 1-2%, good ROAS 3x+

### Google Ads
- **Best for**: High-intent searches, B2B, services
- **Budget start**: $30-50/day minimum
- **Structure**: SKAGs or themed ad groups, 3+ ads per group
- **Targeting**: Start with exact match, expand to phrase
- **KPIs**: CPC, CTR, conversion rate, CPA
- **Benchmarks**: CTR 3-5% search, 0.5% display

### LinkedIn Ads
- **Best for**: B2B, enterprise, recruiting
- **Budget start**: $100/day minimum (expensive)
- **Structure**: Single image + carousel, conversation ads
- **Targeting**: Job title, company size, industry
- **KPIs**: CTR, CPL, lead quality score
- **Benchmarks**: CTR 0.4-0.6%, CPL $50-200

## Content Marketing

### Blog/SEO
- **Best for**: Long-term traffic, authority building
- **Frequency**: 2-4 quality posts/month minimum
- **Content types**: How-to, comparison, listicles, case studies
- **KPIs**: Organic traffic, keyword rankings, time on page
- **Timeline**: 3-6 months to see results

### YouTube
- **Best for**: Education, tutorials, product demos
- **Frequency**: 1-2 videos/week for growth
- **Content types**: Tutorials, reviews, vlogs, shorts
- **KPIs**: Views, watch time, subscribers, CTR
- **Benchmarks**: 4-5% CTR good, 50%+ retention good

### Podcast
- **Best for**: Thought leadership, long-form storytelling
- **Frequency**: Weekly or bi-weekly
- **KPIs**: Downloads, listener retention, reviews
- **Timeline**: 6-12 months to build audience

## Email Marketing

### Welcome Sequence
- Email 1 (Day 0): Welcome + value delivery
- Email 2 (Day 2): Story/mission
- Email 3 (Day 4): Social proof
- Email 4 (Day 7): Soft CTA
- Email 5 (Day 10): Direct offer

### Nurture Campaigns
- **Frequency**: 1-3 emails/week
- **Content mix**: 80% value, 20% promotion
- **KPIs**: Open rate (20%+ good), CTR (2-5% good), unsubscribe (<0.5%)

### Re-engagement
- Trigger: 30-60 days inactive
- Sequence: 3 emails over 2 weeks
- Final email: "We'll remove you" (surprisingly effective)

## Community & Partnerships

### Community Building
- **Platforms**: Discord, Slack, Facebook Groups, Circle
- **Launch with**: 50-100 founding members
- **Engagement**: Daily presence from team initially
- **KPIs**: DAU/MAU ratio, posts per member, retention

### Influencer Marketing
- **Tiers**: Nano (1-10K), Micro (10-100K), Mid (100K-1M), Macro (1M+)
- **Best ROI**: Usually micro-influencers
- **Payment models**: Flat fee, affiliate, product gifting
- **KPIs**: CPM, engagement, conversions, brand lift

### Partnerships
- **Types**: Co-marketing, integrations, referral, affiliate
- **Approach**: Lead with value, propose win-win
- **KPIs**: Referral traffic, joint leads, revenue share

## PR & Launch

### Press Outreach
- **Lead time**: 2-4 weeks before launch
- **Approach**: Personalized pitches, not mass emails
- **Assets needed**: Press kit, founder story, data/insights
- **Targets**: Industry publications, local press, podcasts

### Product Hunt
- **Best day**: Tuesday-Thursday
- **Preparation**: Build hunter network, prepare assets
- **Launch day**: Engage with every comment
- **KPIs**: Ranking, upvotes, traffic spike

### Launch Sequence
1. Week -2: Tease on social
2. Week -1: Email list, close friends
3. Day 0: Full launch, all channels
4. Day 1-3: Engage, respond, amplify
5. Week 1: Press follow-up, case studies
