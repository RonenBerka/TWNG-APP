# Hebrew Luxury Copywriting

Professional guidelines for writing compelling Hebrew copy for luxury goods, jewelry, and premium e-commerce that drives conversions while maintaining brand prestige.

## Core Principles for Hebrew Luxury Copy

### Tone & Voice Characteristics

**Premium Positioning**:
- Sophisticated but accessible - avoid overly flowery or pretentious language
- Confident and authoritative without arrogance
- Warm and personal while maintaining professional distance
- Aspirational yet attainable

**Hebrew-Specific Considerations**:
- Use proper Hebrew grammar and sentence structure (not transliterated English)
- Prefer Biblical/literary Hebrew for elegance over slang or colloquialisms
- Balance modern Israeli Hebrew with timeless expressions
- Avoid mixing Hebrew with English unless brand-specific (minimize "×”×™× ×’×œ×™×©")

### Emotional Triggers for Jewelry & Luxury

**Key Emotional Drivers**:
- **Heritage & Tradition**: Connection to family, legacy, milestones
- **Self-Expression**: Unique identity, personal style, individuality
- **Celebration**: Marking moments, achievements, relationships
- **Craftsmanship**: Artistry, attention to detail, quality
- **Exclusivity**: Limited, special, curated selection
- **Timelessness**: Investment pieces, lasting value
