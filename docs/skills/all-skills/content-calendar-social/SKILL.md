## Content Marketing Philosophy

**Core Principles**:
- Quality over quantity
- Authentic brand storytelling
- Educational and inspirational mix
- Consistent posting schedule
- Community engagement focus
- Visual excellence (jewelry is visual)
- Hebrew-first content

**Content Pillars** (60/30/10 Rule):
- 60%: Value content (education, inspiration, entertainment)
- 30%: Engagement content (questions, polls, UGC)
- 10%: Promotional content (direct sales)

## Content Calendar Structure

### Monthly Planning Framework

**Planning Timeline**:
- Week 4 of previous month: Plan next month's calendar
- Week 1: Prepare content (photoshoots, writing)
- Throughout month: Post according to schedule
- End of month: Analyze performance

**Monthly Content Mix**:
- **Product Showcases**: 8-10 posts (new arrivals, bestsellers)
- **Educational Content**: 5-7 posts (styling tips, care guides)
- **Behind-the-Scenes**: 3-4 posts (craftsmanship, process)
- **Customer Stories**: 3-4 posts (testimonials, UGC)
- **Lifestyle/Inspiration**: 5-7 posts (trends, occasions)
- **Interactive**: 2-3 posts (polls, questions, games)
- **Promotional**: 2-3 posts (sales, offers)

**Total**: ~30-40 feed posts per month (1-1.5 per day)

### Weekly Posting Schedule

**Optimal Posting Times** (Israeli Audience):
- **Best Days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best Times**:
  - Morning: 8:00-10:00 AM (commute scrolling)
  - Lunch: 12:00-2:00 PM (break browsing)
  - Evening: 7:00-9:00 PM (relaxation time)
- **Avoid**: Friday after 2 PM, Saturday (Shabbat)

**Weekly Schedule Template**:

**Sunday**:
- Feed Post: Product spotlight or new arrival
- Stories: Week preview, poll
- Best Time: 8:30 AM or 8:00 PM

**Monday**:
- Feed Post: Motivation/inspiration
- Stories: Behind-the-scenes
- Best Time: 12:30 PM

**Tuesday**:
- Feed Post: Educational (styling tip)
- Stories: Tutorial or how-to
- Best Time: 8:00 AM

**Wednesday**:
- Feed Post: Customer feature or UGC
- Stories: Q&A or quiz
- Best Time: 1:00 PM

**Thursday**:
- Feed Post: Product collection
- Stories: Product details, swipe-up
- Best Time: 8:30 PM

**Friday**:
- Feed Post: Weekend inspiration
- Stories: Shabbat shalom message
- Best Time: 11:00 AM (before Shabbat prep)

**Saturday**:
- No posting (respect Shabbat)
- Schedule for Sunday morning
