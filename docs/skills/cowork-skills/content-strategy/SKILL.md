# Content Strategy Framework

## Content Pillars

| Pillar | Description | Ratio |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| Educational | How-tos, guides, tutorials | 40% |
| Inspirational | Stories, case studies | 25% |
| Promotional | Products, offers, CTAs | 20% |
| Entertaining | Fun, shareable | 15% |

## Content Types by Channel

### Blog/Website
| Type | Length | Frequency | SEO Value |
|------|--------|-----------|-----------|
| How-to guides | 1500-2500 words | 2x/month | High |
| Listicles | 1000-1500 words | 2x/month | Medium |
| Case studies | 1000-2000 words | 1x/month | High |
| Pillar pages | 3000+ words | Quarterly | Very High |

### Social Media
| Platform | Best Length | Frequency |
|----------|-------------|-----------|
| Instagram | 150-200 chars | 1x/day |
| Stories | 15 sec | 3-5x/day |
| Facebook | 80-100 chars | 1x/day |
| LinkedIn | 150-300 chars | 3x/week |

## SEO Checklist

- [ ] Primary keyword in title (first 60 chars)
- [ ] Primary keyword in H1
- [ ] Keywords in first 100 words
- [ ] Image alt text with keywords
- [ ] Internal links (3-5 per article)
- [ ] Meta description (150-160 chars)

## Content Structure Template

```
# [Primary Keyword] Title (H1)
[Hook with keyword]

## What is [Topic]?
## Why [Topic] Matters
## How to [Action]
### Step 1
### Step 2
## Best Practices
## Common Mistakes
## Conclusion + CTA
```

## Performance Metrics

**Blog**: Pageviews, Time on page (>2 min), Bounce rate (<60%)
**Social**: Engagement rate (2-5%), CTR, Shares
**Email**: Open rate (20-30%), Click rate (2-5%)

## Repurposing Matrix

| Original | Repurpose To |
|----------|--------------|
| Blog post | 5-10 social posts |
| Blog post | Email newsletter |
| Blog post | Infographic |
| Webinar | Blog + social clips |
| Case study | Social proof posts |

## Headline Formulas

- "How to [Result] in [Timeframe]"
- "[Number] Ways to [Result]"
- "The Complete Guide to [Topic]"
- "Why [Topic] Will Change Your [Life]"
