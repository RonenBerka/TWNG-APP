## Design Notes for All Emails

### Layout:
- Single column, max width 600px
- Plenty of white space
- Clear hierarchy: headline â†’ body â†’ CTA

### Typography:
- Font: System fonts or web-safe (Arial, Georgia)
- Body: 16px, line-height 1.5
- Headlines: 24-28px

### Colors:
- Background: White or off-white (#FAFAFA)
- Text: Dark gray (#333333)
- Links/Buttons: Brand amber/gold
- Button text: White

### Buttons:
- Rounded corners
- High contrast
- Clear action text ("Start Your Collection" not "Click Here")

### Images:
- Optional hero image in digest emails
- Keep file size small (<100KB)
- Alt text for accessibility

### Mobile:
- All emails must be responsive
- Buttons full-width on mobile
- Font size minimum 14px
