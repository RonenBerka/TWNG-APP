### USA Core Format

**Format varies by era:**

| Era | Format | Example |
|-----|--------|---------|

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. SE vs Core

**Always check:**
- Serial number prefix
- Back of headstock (USA vs Made in Indonesia)
- Hardware quality (weight, feel)
- Pickup covers (USA has PRS logo)

### 2. S2 vs Core

**S2 tells:**
- S2 in serial or model name
- Simplified electronics
- Different tuners
- Lower price point

### 3. 10 Top vs Standard

**10 Top features:**
- "10" on truss rod cover
- More figured maple top
- Same electronics as standard

### 4. McCarty 594 vs Custom 24

**Key differences:**
- Scale length: 594 = 24.594", Custom 24 = 25"
- Pickups: Different voicing
- Sound: 594 more vintage/Gibson-like
