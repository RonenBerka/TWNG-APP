## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. ESP vs E-II vs LTD

**ESP tells:**
- "ESP" alone on headstock
- Japan or USA made
- Premium price ($2,500+)

**E-II tells:**
- "E-II" on headstock
- Japan made
- Mid-premium ($1,500-2,500)

**LTD tells:**
- "LTD" on headstock
- Korea/Indonesia
- Budget to mid ($150-1,500)

### 2. Model Numbers

**LTD numbering:**
- 1000 = Top LTD
- 400 = Mid
- 200 = Entry-mid
- 10-100 = Entry

### 3. Through-Neck Claims

**Verify:**
- True through-neck visible on back
- Premium models only
- Not all ESPs are through-neck
