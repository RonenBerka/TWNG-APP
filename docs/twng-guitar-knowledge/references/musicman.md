## üî¢ Serial Number Decoding

### Music Man USA

**Format: GYYXXXXX or BYYXXXXX**
- G = Guitar
- B = Bass
- YY = Year (2 digits)
- XXXXX = Production number

**Examples:**
| Serial | Decode |
|--------|--------|
| G05123 | Guitar, 2005 |
| B98456 | Bass, 1998 |
| G21001 | Guitar, 2021 |

### Older Format (Pre-2000)

- Various formats
- Check EBMM database

### Sterling by Music Man

| Prefix | Notes |
|--------|-------|
| **S** | Sterling standard |
| **SB** | Sterling bass |
| Year indicators vary |

### Serial Location

- Back of headstock
- Neck plate (some)

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Identification Mistakes

### 1. Music Man vs Sterling

**Music Man tells:**
- "Ernie Ball Music Man" headstock
- USA made
- Premium price ($2,000+)
- 4+2 headstock (guitars)

**Sterling tells:**
- "Sterling by Music Man" headstock
- Indonesia (mostly)
- Lower price ($300-900)
- Similar designs

### 2. StingRay Versions

**StingRay Classic:**
- 2-band EQ
- Vintage style

**StingRay Special:**
- 3-band EQ
- Neodymium pickup
- Modern features

### 3. Sterling SUB vs Ray

**SUB:**
- Entry level
- Simplified electronics
- $300-400

**Ray34/35:**
- Premium Sterling
- Better hardware
- $600-900
