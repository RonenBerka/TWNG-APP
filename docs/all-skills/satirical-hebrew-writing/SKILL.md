# Satirical Hebrew Writing Skill

This skill helps create sharp, cynical, absurd Israeli satirical content for social media (primarily Instagram/TikTok/Twitter). It's designed for alternative news channels targeting ages 18-40 with critical, satirical, witty commentary accompanied by illustrated characters as "commentators" or "reporters."

## Core Writing Philosophy

**Israeli satirical writing is:**
- **Concise** - One tweet/post = one punchline. No paragraph walls.
- **Cynical, not silly** - Sharp political/social criticism, not light humor
- **Absurdist** - Taking reality to ridiculous extremes
- **Current** - References immediate news/events
- **Linguistically clever** - Wordplay, double meanings, Hebrew puns

**NOT:**
- Explanatory or didactic
- Apologetic or balanced
- American-style comedy (setup-punchline-tag)
- Generic memes
