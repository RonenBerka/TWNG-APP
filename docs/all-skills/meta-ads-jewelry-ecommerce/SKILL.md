# Meta Ads for Jewelry E-commerce

Strategic framework for creating, optimizing, and scaling Meta (Facebook & Instagram) advertising campaigns for jewelry and luxury e-commerce businesses.

## Campaign Structure Framework

### Funnel-Based Campaign Organization

**Awareness Campaigns** (Top of Funnel):
- **Objective**: Reach, Brand Awareness
- **Audience**: Cold, broad interests
- **Creative**: Brand story, collection showcases, lifestyle content
- **Budget**: 20-30% of total ad spend
- **KPIs**: CPM, Reach, Video Views, Engagement Rate

**Consideration Campaigns** (Middle of Funnel):
- **Objective**: Traffic, Engagement
- **Audience**: Engaged audiences, website visitors (1-30 days), lookalikes
- **Creative**: Product highlights, customer testimonials, educational content
- **Budget**: 30-40% of total ad spend
- **KPIs**: CTR, CPC, Page Views, Add to Cart

**Conversion Campaigns** (Bottom of Funnel):
- **Objective**: Conversions (Purchase)
- **Audience**: Cart abandoners, product viewers, engaged shoppers
- **Creative**: Product-specific, urgency, social proof, offers
- **Budget**: 40-50% of total ad spend
- **KPIs**: ROAS, CPA, Conversion Rate, Purchase Value

### Campaign Naming Convention

**Format**: `[Stage]_[Product]_[Audience]_[Format]_[Date]`

Example:
- `CONV_Rings_CartAbandon_Carousel_Dec24`
- `AWARE_Collection_BroadInt_Video_Dec24`
- `CONSID_Necklaces_LAL1%_Static_Dec24`

## Audience Targeting Strategy

### Interest-Based Targeting (Cold Audiences)

**Primary Jewelry Interests**:
- Jewelry design
- Fine jewelry brands (Tiffany, Cartier, Pandora)
- Fashion magazines (Vogue, Elle)
- Luxury lifestyle
- Wedding planning
- Anniversary gifts
- Fashion weeks

**Behavioral Targeting**:
- Online shoppers
- Engaged shoppers
- Luxury goods shoppers
- Anniversary within 30/60/90 days
- Upcoming birthday

**Demographic Layers**:
- Age: 25-65 (adjust based on product price point)
- Gender: Typically women for jewelry (test both)
- Income: Top 10-25% for luxury
- Relationship status: Engaged, Married (for certain products)

### Custom Audiences (Warm/Hot)

**Website-Based**:
- All website visitors (180 days)
- Product page viewers (30/60/90 days)
- Category browsers (14/30 days)
- Add to cart (7/14 days)
- Checkout initiated (3/7 days)
- Past purchasers (365 days for upsell/cross-sell)

**Engagement-Based**:
- Instagram profile visitors
- Instagram engaged users (90 days)
- Facebook page engaged
- Video viewers (25%, 50%, 75%, 95%)
- Lead form openers

**Customer List**:
- Email subscribers
- CRM customer database
- Exclude recent purchasers (0-30 days)

### Lookalike Audiences

**Seed Audiences** (in order of quality):
1. Top 25% purchasers by value (1-2% LAL)
2. All purchasers 365 days (1-3% LAL)
3. Add to cart 60 days (2-4% LAL)
4. Product page viewers 90 days (3-5% LAL)
5. Website visitors 180 days (5-8% LAL)

**Israeli Market Consideration**:
- Use Israel-specific seed audiences
- Test regional expansion (similar markets) if scaling
- Hebrew-speaking audiences globally

## Creative Strategy

### Ad Format Selection

**Carousel Ads** (Best for Jewelry):
- Showcase multiple products or angles
- Tell product story across cards
- Highlight different features (design, materials, wearing)
- 3-5 cards optimal
- Lead with strongest product/image

**Single Image Ads**:
- Hero product shots
- Lifestyle imagery
- Testimonial graphics
- Clean, minimal distractions

**Video Ads**:
- Product 360Â° views
- Close-up details and sparkle
- Customer unboxing
- Behind-the-scenes craftsmanship
- 6-15 seconds optimal for feed
- 30-60 seconds for Stories

**Collection Ads**:
- Showcase product catalog
- Enable direct shopping
- Great for retargeting with dynamic product ads

### Visual Best Practices for Jewelry

**Photography Requirements**:
- High resolution (minimum 1080x1080)
- Consistent lighting and backgrounds
- Show product details (close-ups)
- Include lifestyle/on-model shots
- White or neutral backgrounds for product shots
- Contextual backgrounds for lifestyle

**Composition Guidelines**:
- Product takes 60-80% of frame
- Clear, uncluttered backgrounds
- Text overlay minimal (<20% of image)
- Brand logo subtle, not dominant
- Show scale (on hand/body when appropriate)

**Video Specifications**:
- First 3 seconds crucial - lead with product
- Show product sparkle and movement
- Include captions (80% watch without sound)
- Aspect ratios: 1:1 (feed), 9:16 (Stories/Reels)
- Resolution: 1080p minimum

## Budget & Bidding Strategy

### Starting Budget Recommendations

**Total Monthly Budget**:
- Minimum: â‚ª3,000-5,000 for testing
- Small business: â‚ª5,000-15,000
- Established: â‚ª15,000-50,000+
- Scale based on ROAS performance

**Campaign Budget Allocation**:
- Awareness: 20-30%
- Consideration: 30-40%
- Conversion: 40-50%
- Retargeting: 30-40% of total (overlaps with conversion)

**Daily Budgets**:
- Minimum â‚ª50-100 per ad set for stability
- Allow 2-3x CPA for learning phase
- Don't spread budget too thin across ad sets

### Bidding Strategy

**Learning Phase** (First 50 conversions/week):
- Use "Lowest Cost" or "Highest Volume"
- Let algorithm learn
- Don't make changes for 3-5 days
- Expect higher CPA initially

**Optimization Phase**:
- Shift to "Cost Cap" with target CPA
- Or "Bid Cap" for strict control
- "ROAS Target" when sufficient data (100+ purchases)

**Advanced Scaling**:
- Increase budget 20% every 3-5 days (if performing)
- Duplicate winning ad sets (CBO for scaling)
- Test "Campaign Budget Optimization" vs. ad set budgets

## Performance Optimization

### Key Metrics to Monitor

**Campaign Level**:
- **ROAS** (Return on Ad Spend) - Target: 3-5x for mature campaigns
- **CPA** (Cost Per Acquisition) - Benchmark against product margins
- **Frequency** - Keep under 3-4 for cold audiences
- **CPM** - Monitor for fatigue or competition changes

**Ad Set Level**:
- **CTR** (Click-Through Rate) - Target: 1.5%+ for cold, 3%+ for warm
- **CPC** (Cost Per Click) - Lower is better, compare to industry
- **Conversion Rate** - Track landing page performance
- **Add to Cart Rate** - Benchmark against site average

**Creative Level**:
- **Engagement Rate** - Likes, comments, shares
- **Video View Rate** - 25%, 50%, 75% thresholds
- **Hook Rate** - First 3 seconds retention
- **Click-to-Conversion** - Landing page effectiveness

### Testing Framework

**What to Test** (One variable at a time):

**Audience Testing**:
- Interest categories (fashion vs. luxury lifestyle)
- Age ranges (25-34 vs. 35-44 vs. 45-65)
- Lookalike percentages (1% vs. 3% vs. 5%)
- Stacked interests vs. broad

**Creative Testing**:
- Image styles (white background vs. lifestyle)
- Video vs. static images
- Product angles and compositions
- Carousel vs. single image
- With/without models

**Copy Testing**:
- Emotional vs. feature-focused
- Long vs. short copy
- Different CTAs and headlines
- Urgency vs. value messaging
- Emoji usage variations

**Offer Testing**:
- Free shipping vs. % discount
- Gift with purchase
- Bundle offers
- No offer (value-based only)

### Optimization Checklist

**Daily**:
- Check spend pacing
- Review ROAS/CPA trends
- Pause underperforming ads (ROAS <1.5x after spending 2x CPA)
- Monitor frequency (pause if >4)

**Weekly**:
- Analyze top performers and scale
- Refresh creative (every 2-3 weeks)
- Test new audiences
- Adjust budgets based on performance
- Review landing page metrics

**Monthly**:
- Comprehensive performance review
- Update product catalog
- Refresh seasonal messaging
- Analyze customer feedback
- Competitive analysis
