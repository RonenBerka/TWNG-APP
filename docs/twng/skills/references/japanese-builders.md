## üîç Identifying Japanese Guitars

### "Made in Japan" Value
- Often equal to USA quality
- 1970s-80s especially valued
- Lower labor cost = better specs
- "MIJ" = quality signal

### Serial Number Prefixes
| Prefix | Factory |
|--------|---------|
| **F** | Fujigen |
| **T** | Tokai |
| **A** | Various |
| **J** | Japan general |

### Lawsuit Era (1970s)
| Brand | Copied |
|-------|--------|
| Tokai | Gibson, Fender |
| Greco | Gibson, Fender |
| Burny | Gibson |
| Fernandes | Fender |
| Aria | Various |

**Note:** "Lawsuit" is somewhat mythical - few actual lawsuits occurred. Term refers to era of high-quality copies.
