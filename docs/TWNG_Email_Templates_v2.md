## Design Notes

### Tone Shift from v1:
| Before | After |
|--------|-------|
| "Join the community" | "Your collection" |
| "Share with others" | "Keep it safe" |
| "Connect with collectors" | "Private by default" |
| "Document your guitars" | "A place for your guitars" |
| "Tell your story" | "Tell its story" (guitar's story) |

### Layout:
- Single column, 600px max
- Generous white space
- Short paragraphs

### CTAs:
- "Add Your First Guitar"
- "Go to Your Collection"
- "Review Your Collection"
- Never "Join" or "Share"

### Footer:
- Always include unsubscribe
- Tagline: Every Guitar Has a Story
- No heavy social pressure
